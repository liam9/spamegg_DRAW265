Dragon Curve Users Manual

What is the set of all SVG images which can be generated by Dragon Curve?

The Dragon Curve application can generate an SVG image of a shape consisting of a series of line segments connected by 90 degree rotations. A recursive pattern of left and right 90 degree rotations creates a shape known as the Dragon Curve.

The user can chose the number of iterations, the length of the lines and the colour. Although any number of iterations is possible, the run-time becomes very long at large numbers of iterations.

There are special words that can be entered as colours in order to create different effects:

  • "Fun" shifts the colour with each iteration

  • "Pieces" changes colour of sections divided into equal size

  • "Growing_Pieces" changes colour of sections that grow exponentialy in size

  • "Combine" changes colour every hundred lines

  • "Mix" changes colour every third line
The simple Dragon Curve can also be transformed in a number of ways:

  • It can overlap scaled down copies and shift its colour a maximum of 5 times to create a mesh like pattern

  • It can be coppied any number of times and rotated by any angle around the origin

  • It can be coppied with each copy being spun and have its colour changed

  • Dragon 1 This image contains a Dragon Curve consisting of 8 iterations, has line length of 12, its the colour blue. The Dragon Curve was then duplicated 5 times and scaled down by a factor of 0.7 each time to create a mesh pattern.

  • Dragon 2 This image contains a Dragon Curves consisting of 14 iterations, has line length of 2 and it is the colour 'Combine'. The dragon was then duplicated 8 times and rotated around the origin by an angle of 2*pi/8

  • Dragon 3 This image contains a Dragon Curves consisting of 10 iterations, has line length of 2 and it is the colour 'Fun'. The dragon was then duplicated 6 times and rotated around the origin by an angle of 2*pi/6

  • Dragon 4 This image contains a Dragon Curves consisting of 14 iterations, has line length of 1 and it is the colour 'Pieces'. The dragon was then trandsformed by dragon spin

What applications are supported by Dragon Curve?

Dragon Curve supports four application: simple_dragon_curve.sh, mesh_dragon_curve.sh, ring_dragon_curve.sh, dragon_spin.sh.

To invoke simple_dragon_curve.sh from the Linux command line: 

	bash simple_dragon_curve.sh number_of_dragons iterations line_length colour

To invoke mesh_dragon_curve.sh from the Linux command line: 

	bash mesh_dragon_curve.sh iterations line_length colour shadow_scale amount

To invoke ring_dragon_curve.sh from the Linux command line: 

	bash ring_dragon_curve.sh interations line_length colour amount angle

ring_dragon_curve.sh can also be invoked with a text file as input: 

	bash ring_dragon_curve.sh file_name.txt amount angle

To invoke dragon_spin.sh from the Linux command line: 

	bash dragon_spin.sh interations line_length colour

What shell commands are used to generate the SVG images above?

To generate Dragon 0: 
	bash simple_dragon_curve.sh 12 3 Black
To generate Dragon 1: 
	bash mesh_dragon_curve.sh 8 12 Blue 0.7 5
To generate Dragon 2: 
	bash ring_dragon_curve.sh 14 2 Combine 8 0.7853981633
To generate Dragon 3: 
	bash ring_dragon_curve.sh 10 2 Fun 6 1.0471975512
To generate Dragon 4: 
	bash dragon_spin.sh 14 1 Pieces
